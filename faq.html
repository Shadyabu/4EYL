What is an elder concierge?

 

The Executive Concierges at 4Ever Young Living provides comprehensive options and guidance on senior living that’s tailored to the individual.
All 4Ever Young Living clients have access to our team of highly proficient and knowledgeable in-home and transitional care specialists.
In addition, they are able to utilize our preferred partner list that has been vetted to include only those companies that provide our valued clients the time and attention they need.




What are the costs involved?

 

4Ever Young Living is pleased to extend a complimentary assessment and consultation to all prospective clients. Following a thorough review of the individual's care requirements, our team devises a tailored care plan, with costs varying according to needs. Each service provided by 4Ever Young Living is distinct and, in certain instances, may be offered to families at no charge.





Who typically hires elder care concierge services?

 

Our clients are most often mature adults, children of mature adults, baby boomers, relatives and friends looking for solutions and information on high-quality care services, and up-to-date options.








What other services does 4Ever Young Living provide?

 

As a private elder care concierge, our goal is to be an advocate for all things related to mature living. Our primary services encompass Home-care, Placement, and Long Term Care Insurance Claims. Additionally, we collaborate with trusted partners who specialize in areas beyond our core offerings.


What geographical area does 4Ever Young Living serve?

 

Although our headquarters are located in Orange County, we extend our services throughout all of Southern California.





Does 4Ever Young Living work with clients in care facilities?

 

Our in-home care specialists provide one-on-one care, companion care, and transitional care to skilled nursing settings, independent living facilities, assisted living communities, memory care communities, and residential care facilities for the elderly (RCFE).






Can 4Ever Young Living work with clients in care facilities?

 

We provide transitional care and offer support in coordinating moves for seniors. Through our list of vetted partners. Our team is able to provide a plan of action for each part of the moving process.





Does 4Ever Young Living work with clients in care facilities?

 

4Ever Young Living provides all clients with access to our preferred partner list which includes transportation services.





After initial contact, how quickly can services start?

 

Services can begin immediately after an initial discovery call with our team.
