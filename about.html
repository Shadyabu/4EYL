Our History

 

4Ever Young Living was founded as a way to help people of all ages achieve happiness and reach their goals by providing a level of care that differentiates and elevates the company among other industry specialists. Reema Khetarpal, founder and principal, brings over twenty years of experience to the company, which she started over a decade ago.Reema is certified in long-term care (cltc) and is an expert in her field: aging in place. Her company, 4Ever Young Living, is a leader in the senior care industry serving southern california, having helped over 3500 families.

WHAT WE DO

 

4Ever Young Living provides all clients access to the highest quality of care and resources available within the elder care industry. Our team of specialists has the ability and desire to deliver comprehensive solutions and guidance on senior living options. The client’s needs are always the main focus of the 4Ever Young Living team. We will work with you and your family to ensure all your needs are met so you can get back to what is important – living your life.

What Sets Us Apart

 

Compassion

Experience the warmth of family, with personalized care that lifts spirits and eases burdens. See the joy in your loved one's eyes when they receive compassionate, in-home care that prioritizes well-being.

Expertise

Our experienced team navigates elder care with confidence, ensuring quality care, tailored solutions, and a brighter future. Our knowledge and experience empower seniors to thrive, maximizing independence and enhancing well-being.

Peace of Mind

Taking care of a loved one’s health can be stressful and draining. Our process allows those we serve to feel a great deal of comfort from our specialists based on their experience and familiarity of the industry.
